Who We Are

We’re a small team of four creative storytellers who believe every child’s imagination deserves its own spotlight.

Together, we craft personalised stories based on your chosen details — from favourite colours and pets to hobbies and magical worlds. Each book is written with heart, laughter, and a sprinkle of imagination.

Meet the Dream Pages Team

Vicky “The Word Weaver”

Head Story Designer

“Hi, I’m Vicky! I’m the one who turns your child’s dreams into magical adventures. I’ve always believed that every child deserves to see themselves as the hero of their own story. I love rainy days, big mugs of tea, and fantasy novels that make me forget time. Fun fact? I once wrote an entire bedtime story in rhyme — completely by accident!"

My motto: ‘Every story starts with a spark — I just fan the flames.’

Anna “The Color Whisperer”

Lead Illustrator

“Hello! I’m Anna, the illustrator who brings your stories to life with color and imagination. I love creating worlds where every page feels like a dream. I’m happiest with a watercolor brush in hand, a cat nearby, and bubble tea within reach. I secretly name every character I draw — even the clouds!"

My motto: ‘If you can imagine it, I can draw it.’

Patric “The Page Architect”

Design & Layout Artist

“Hey there, I’m Patric — the layout perfectionist around here. My job is to make sure every page looks beautiful and flows just right. I love clean designs, soft paper textures, and movie soundtracks while I work. I can’t stand misaligned margins — they haunt my dreams!"

My motto: ‘Design is the art of invisible structure.’

Zack “The Story Alchemist”

Editor & Story Coach

“Hi, I’m Zack! I polish every story until it sparkles. I make sure every word, sentence, and rhythm feels just right for our little readers. I love old libraries, adventure games, and (probably too much) coffee. A funny fact — I still have the very first book I ever edited. It’s full of crayon doodles!"

My motto: ‘Every word can sparkle — it just needs the right polish.’”
