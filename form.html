Delivery & Returns Information
Delivery
  • Each personalised storybook is created individually for your child.
  • Production usually takes 7–10 days after receiving all details and your approval of the draft.
  • You will receive a digital version (PDF) by email first.
  • If you order a printed copy, delivery time depends on your location:
    • UK & Europe: 5–10 business days
    • Worldwide: 10–20 business days
  • You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been shipped.
Returns & Replacements

Because every book is custom-made and unique, we are unable to accept general returns or exchanges.

However, we always want you to be happy with your story!

We will offer a free replacement or digital correction if:

  • There’s a printing error or damaged item on arrival.
  • We’ve made a mistake in your personalised details.

If you notice an issue, please contact us within 7 days of receiving your order at [your email address] and include your order number and a short description (or a photo, if relevant).

Cancellations

You may cancel your order within 24 hours of placing it — before we begin creating your personalised story. Once the story design has started, cancellation may not be possible.

Privacy Policy

At Dream Pages, we care deeply about your privacy and the safety of your child’s information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you share with us.

  1. Information We Collect

    We collect only the information needed to create your personalised storybook, including:

    • Parent or guardian’s name and contact details
    • Child’s first name, age, and favourite things

  2. How We Use the Information

    Your data is used only to create your child’s personalised storybook.

  3. How We Store and Protect Data
